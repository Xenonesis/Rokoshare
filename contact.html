The RokoShare app is currently in development and will be launching soon. We're working hard to bring you the best shared mobility experience. Stay tuned for updates and be the first to know when we go live!

We'll be covering major sectors and business districts in Gurugram with fixed routes connecting residential areas to commercial hubs. Our initial coverage will include Cyber City, Golf Course Road, Sohna Road, and major residential sectors.

Driver partnership opportunities will be available once we launch. We're looking for reliable, professional drivers with valid licenses and auto-rickshaws. Please contact us for more information about joining our driver network and earning opportunities.
