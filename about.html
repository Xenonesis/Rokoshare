About RokoShare

Revolutionizing shared mobility in Gurugram

Our Mission

Transforming Urban Mobility

At RokoShare, we believe that daily commuting should be affordable, reliable, and stress-free. Our mission is to transform the way people travel in Gurugram by providing shared auto rides on fixed routes at predictable prices.

We're committed to reducing traffic congestion, lowering transportation costs, and creating a more sustainable urban mobility solution for working professionals and students.
